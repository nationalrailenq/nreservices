    Website and knowledgebase

    • Comprehensive 'one-stop' shop for information about travelling by rail
    • Real-time journey planning
    • Real-time live departure boards
    • Service disruption / engineering work information
    • Comprehensive internal Knowledgebase for Contact Centre and TOC staff

    Real-time journey planning

    • Plan your journey up to 12 weeks in advance
    • Get real time updates for information about delays affecting journeys
    • Re-plan journeys affected by disruption
    • Plan from or to your 'Home' or 'Work' stations

    Live and historical departure boards

    • Powered by Darwin and updated in real-time
    • Accurate information about train movements across the country
    • In disruption, additional station-level information is added by the NRCC
    • View the last 90 days' of train movements
    • Enables customer relations staff to investigate claims
    • Includes a trace of where information was received from and when

    Darwin data feed and workstation

    • Harness the power of the Darwin database
    • The single source of real-time train movement information
    • Available for interface into your systems according to your requirements
    • Comprehensive train data management tool via the workstation
    • Up to 500 trains can be amended / cancelled in one transaction with fast and simple creation of additional trains
    • Tomorrow's information today, information can be manipulated up to 48 hours in advance

    Live departure boards for staff

    • Powered by Darwin and updated in real-time
    • Headcodes and intermediate passing points are visible together with an extended lookup period
    • Available as a mobile version for on train and station staff

    Service disruptions and engineering works (with XML)

    • Details of incidents affecting the rail network are published on the National Rail Enquiries website
    • Contains details of the problem, impact and advice of alternative options
    • Updated in real-time 24/7, reviewed and updated every 20 minutes as per the industry standard
    • Can be provided via an XML feed direct to TOC or 3rd party websites
    • Information is consistent with what’s on our website
    • XML feed of disruption information updated 24/7

    Promotions

    • XML feed of current ticket promotions available on the UK railway
    • Information is consistent with what’s on our website
    • You can tailor the presentation to your company's requirements

    Severe incident messages

    • Get emailed updates about disruption
    • Updated 24/7 by the NRCC
    • Includes internal information for railway staff

    Cheapest fare finder

    • Find the lowest fares for your journey
    • Choose the train company you wish to travel with
    • Tweak the dates or times to find even cheaper fares

    Season ticket calculator

    • Input your journey details
    • Choose how you want to travel, either by First or Standard Class
    • Our calculator returns the available tickets, with options travelling on different routes where available

    Ticket hand-offs

    • Receive sales leads for passengers looking to book travel tickets
    • Includes origin and destination locations, journey times
    • Also includes passenger numbers and any railcard selections

    Advertising

    • National Rail Enquiries website reaches over 7 million UK adults each month
    • These users are actively sourcing information prior to making purchase decisions

    Webservices

    • Develop your own application using the same data used on our website
    • Both Journey Planning and live departures feeds available

    Widgets (Mac) and Gadgets (Windows)

    • Live departures for your chosen station available on your desktop
    • Available branded with your company's logo

    Mobile apps - iPhone, Android, Windows Phone 7

    • Access up-to-minute train time information when you need it
    • View live train progress and platform information (where available)
    • Quickly find your nearest station, see directions on the iPhone maps and then find your next trains home!

    Alerts

    • Get alerted about problems on your journeys direct by SMS, email or Twitter direct message
    • Sign-up for your regular journeys
    • Choose your thresholds for when you want to be notified

    Train Tracker and Train Tracker Text

    • Voice activated journey planning tool
    • Links through to our real time train information for accurate journey plans
    • You can have your own branding, with different numbers / voice artists
    • Text us your journey details and we’ll reply straight away
    • Live departures or arrivals for your chosen station
    • Includes information about platforms and disruptions

    CIS screens

    • Presents live departure boards information in a CIS-style format
    • Can be presented with TOC branding
    • Simple web interface allows the information to be integrated into other devices such as help points

    Text Me My Journey Plan

    • Plan your journey through our online Journey Planner
    • Receive your train times direct to your phone

    Social media - Facebook, Twitter

    • Become a fan of National Rail Enquiries on Facebook
    • Add our “Visit your friend” app to plan journeys to friends and events
    • Keep up to date with our initiatives
    • Real time disruption updates 24/7
    • Subscribe by your preferred train company

    Pocket timetable

    • Input your journey details and when you like to travel
    • Our tool creates a printable timetable according to your request
    • Available as a white label product to fit your brand

    Outlook Add-In

    • Plan journeys to meetings direct from Outlook Calendar
    • Blocks out the travelling time in your calendar
    • Link through to website to check fares and buy tickets

    Ticket conditions

    • XML feed of information about each ticket type and its validity
    • Shows which train company certain tickets are valid with
    • Includes info about booking restrictions / refunds

    Station information

    • Ensure your website is up to date with the latest station information
    • From ticket office opening hours to whether there's a cash machine, it's all there

    Call answering

    • An expert information and advisory service to customers for all train services in Great Britain
    • We provide timetable and fares information, booking information, refund information, details of special offers, Travelcard and Oyster information, as well as a host of other train travel-related products

    Customer feedback

    • A dedicated team responding to customer enquiries, feedback and complaints through a variety of methods
    • Get in touch with us via email, webform, phone and white mail
    • Information to help understand what your customers are telling you about your service

    Telesales transfer

    • A one-stop shop service for customers
    • Our Contact Centre provides up-to-date fares and availability information
    • Facility to transfer customers directly to telesales
